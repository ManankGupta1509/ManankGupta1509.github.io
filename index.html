Story Time

A Cute Little Love Story
  • By Shannu Gupta

We remember the stories you tell us about the first time you met each other. It was bright sunny day when Mom you were just starting to begin your day with Nannu and Nanaji. And then Nannu spotted two handsome looking bachelors approaching to the house. There was Dad with his brother. The love came to both of you by looking at the first sight. You and Dad knew so perfectly well that you’ve found the soulmate for life. And we don’t doubt it a bit. We’ve seen you grow together and become one. Somebody would know what an old school romance would look like by just looking at you. You both blend so perfectly together.

Story of Corona Pandemic
  • By your Three Musketeers

“We will fight anything until we are in it together “. Yes we go by this motto. Where the whole world is fighting this pandemic I’m so happy that we’re in this together. Every single day we’re so grateful to wake up in the morning with you. Papa the way you wake all of us up with a glass of water in your hand, trust me, it has become a habit. None of our day can begin without drinking a glass of water and that too coming from you. The extent you have spoiled has no bounds. It’s our wishes and a command for you. You don’t even know how to get angry. And that’s is the sweetest part about you. Mumma, what to say, it’s just hard to spend a day without listening to your taunts. You’ve mastered it so well after all. I just love our chat sessions. And I absolutely adore how we all can manipulate you to not work and cherish a gossip time. You’re always ready to give the pep talk. And dancing with you is always so much fun. I can go on endlessly here and I know I will always run out of the words to describe how these 25 years have become a memory to cherish for lifetime. You both are what dreams are made of.

Journey Of 25 years
  • By Family

It all began at the onset of the winter in December in the year 1995. A typical Indian match-made set in 90s followed by an ordinary marriage. People get married everyday however this was special because it was going to be the start of something so beautiful and so pure.
It has been 25 years since then. Matches are made in heaven and so were yours. You set a beautiful example of how two ordinary human beings can come together and create an extraordinary life for not only themselves but for others too.
Been through all the ups and downs, I must be exaggerating by saying this but you made the life a cakewalk. From helping each other in the kitchen to making the life changing decisions.
